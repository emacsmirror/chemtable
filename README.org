* chemtable.el

Periodic table of elements.

** Installation:
   
After installing the package (or copying it to your load-path), add this
to your init file:

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(require 'chemtable)
#+end_src

** Usage:
   
~M-x chemtable~ and click or RET on element.
